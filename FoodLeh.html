Details

Our website aims to provide a centralized platform for smaller F&B owners to list their stalls, or for a cloud kitchen facility to utilize such that they can dispatch the orders across all of their food vendors located within its premises. Through this, customers can use it to order food for self-collection. A dashboard is also provided to the owners to manage their orders and also view the statistics of their orders. Lastly, we provide a service where orders from customers can be sent to the owners Whatsapp account for owners who are less tech-savvy.
