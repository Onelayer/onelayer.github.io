Details

Our group would like to help auto insurance companies with the detection of fraudulent practices through both supervised and unsupervised machine learning models. Models will classify different insurance claim records into “fraud” and “non-fraud” classes based on vehicles’ characteristics, accident details and policy details that are provided in the training dataset. We believe that through these models, auto insurers will be able to identify fraudulent applications more accurately and avoid any potential losses.
